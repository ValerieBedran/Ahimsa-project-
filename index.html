Current project

"Raw"

At the age of 51 Janette Murray-Wakelin was diagnosed with highly aggressive carcinoma breast cancer and was told she had 6 months to live. 6 months latter Janette was completely clean. She switched to a 100% raw diet, started to run marathons, set a World Record of 366 consecutive Marathons in one year, gained 40 000 followers all over the world and inspired Brunei government to change state nutrition policy. Now Janette is 68, her husband Alan Murray is 73. They feel themselves stronger every day and keep beating world records.
